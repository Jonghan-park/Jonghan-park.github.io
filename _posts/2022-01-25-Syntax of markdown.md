- Heading
# Heading1  > "# Heading" or "========="
## Heading2 > "## Heading" or "--------"
### Heading3 > "### Heading"
#### Heading4 > "#### Heading"
##### Heading5 > "##### Heading"
###### Heading6 > "###### Heading"

___

- Line
"___" < Three Underscores 

___

- Line breaks
To create a new line, put two or more spaces at the end.

First line.  
Second line.

___


- Bold text
Put ** or __ before and after a word.
This is a **test** text.
This is a __test__ text.
